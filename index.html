Hungry Hands

Food ordering app for csun students

Hungry Hands-is a Food ordering app, that makes the ordering process at school much simpler. The goal in mind was to make an very simple user interface that allow any user from school order food items from the restaurants on campus. The app will constantly display different restaurants in the area, so users can try different restaurants, see its menus and try out their food. This app is designed to work on multiple platforms and have live updates from restaurants available in our app.
